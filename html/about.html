Vision - Mission - Value

Vision

To become best-in-class in the education and training industry. We do this through our commitment to deliver life-changing learning experiences that empower our students for life.

Mission

We are committed to continuously improve our operations, to build more schools and to provide 21st century learning standard for our students.

Core values

  1. We place our customer experience at the core of all we do.
  2. We strive continually to grow, learn and improve.
  3. We give back to our Community.
  4. We operate our business ethically, transparently and efﬁciently.
  5. We are committed to build a professional and open working environment, where people have equal chance to grow.

Where we are located?

Ho Chi Minh

About Ho Chi Minh City

HCMC is the biggest city in Vietnam and a cosmopolitan hub.The city is a vibrant mix of traditional and modern inﬂuences, with an incredible array of street food. There is a large international community spread across the many administrative districts.HCMC is conveniently located for travel both to the southern beach towns of Vietnam and international travel to Cambodia, Thailand, Singapore and Malaysia.

Ho Chi Minh ILA Centre

ILA currently has 21 centres across the city as well as our public school programme. Our corporate Head Ofﬁce is also located here.Ho Chi Minh City is the main location for ILA schools, with centres spread around the city in different districts. From the biggest to the smallest, ILA schools are all professional centres with an enthusiastic group of teachers and staff dedicated to bringing quality English language teaching to students.

Vung Tau

About Vung Tau City

The beautiful beach town of Vung Tau is famous for its laid back lifestyle, friendly locals and miles of sandy beaches.

You can watch the sun rise and set with awesome sea views, the air is clean and the traffic is light. Life in Vung Tau is easy-going and laid-back. It offers a wide range of outdoor activities, including cycling, swimming, relaxing on the beach and exploring the beautiful and at times untouched local countryside.

Vung Tau ILA Center

The ILA centre of Vung Tau is well known in the community and is bright, spacious and has a great working atmosphere. Enjoy a relaxed beach lifestyle whilst working at one of the premier language centres in Vietnam.

Come here if you want to live by the beach ,you love eating fresh seafood

Da Nang

About Da Nang City

Da Nang is a coastal destination with a number of resorts populating its white sandy beaches. Da Nang is well known for its laid back vibe and is a nice balance between a friendly small town and large well-connected city.

Da Nang ILA Center

ILA Da Nang is centrally located, close to the town centre and the beach, and provides teachers with a friendly, fun and professional work environment. A short distance from Da Nang are Hoi An, a UNESCO world heritage sight and a living museum of traditional Vietnamese architecture; and Hue, home to the French colonial capital and the tombs of the Nguyen Emperors.

Because of it’s low turnover ILA Da Nang only takes internal transfers or part time local candidates.

Ha Noi

About Ha Noi City

Vietnam's unique and charming capital city is an evocative and at times chaotic mix of stunning French colonial buildings, ancient temple complexes, leafy tree lined streets and lakes and modern and colourful shops and houses. Each quarter has its own distinctive flair, from the bustling old quarter with merchants peddling their goods in its narrow lanes, to the diplomatic quarter with embassies and ministry buildings along tree lined boulevards. The city is known for its cuisine, and locals delight in explaining how vastly superior their food is to that of the rest of Vietnam.

Ha Noi ILA Center

In Hanoi, ILA has been expanding rapidly, and is now our second largest location. As in all ILA centres, teachers can expect continued professional development opportunities, a social working environment, as well as great resources and support from the academic management.

About Hai Phong City

Close to the natural beauty of Ha Long Bay in northern Vietnam, Hai Phong is one of the country’s fastest growing cities. Can Tho

About Can Tho City

Can Tho is Vietnam’s fourth largest city and the hub of the Mekong Delta. Located on the southern bank of the Hau River, Can Tho is most known for its floating market where vendors sell fresh local produce from their boats. The city has a busy riverside tourist area that includes many restaurants where you can eat tasty local delicacies for very cheap prices. While Can Tho is a city where you can relaxed way of life in the Mekong Delta, it is also a quickly developing city with new shopping malls, supermarkets, hotels, gyms, cinemas, etc. Can Tho is located 170 km southeast from Ho Chi Minh City and is linked by frequent bus services. In 2011, a new airport opened that offers domestic flights as well as limited flights to Bangkok and Taiwan.

Rach Gia

About Rach Gia City

Rạch Giá is a provincial city and the capital city of Kien Giang Province, Vietnam. It is located on the eastern coast of the Gulf of Thailand, 250 kilometres south-west of Ho Chi Minh City. East of city, it borders Tân Hiệp and Châu Thành town, the Gulf of Thailand is to the west and surrounds some parts of the city, south of the city is Châu Thành and An Biên town, northward it borders Hòn Đất and Tân Hiệp.

Currently, Rach Gia Airport is the biggest airport in Rạch Giá. Rạch Giá airport has flight routes to Phú Quốc and Ho Chi Minh City with Vietnam Airlines.

Rạch Giá is a very authentic Vietnamese city with few tourists. Children here are still excited to see a foreigner and walking on the streets you will be stopped all the time with people saying hello. Although primarily used by tourists as a transfer point to Phu Quoc, the city itself is quite nice and accommodation is abundant and cheap.
